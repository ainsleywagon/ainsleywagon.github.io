I have always loved color and balancing things visually. As a kid, I obsessed over the perfect pairing of pink & green and arranged M&Ms in rainbow order. Since first meeting Adobe Illustrator in 2008, I’ve been making vector graphics and figuring out how to put them on the internet.

I got my undergrad degree in Architecture and had the chance to explore product design, play with arduinos, and give a lecture about haptic installations. I wanted to design brighter, iterate faster, and have more control over the final product so I gravitated toward designing digital products on the web.

With particular experience working on small teams, I love shaping the visual direction of the design as well as the details of its implementation. Last year I was a Code for America fellow working with Puerto Rico and spent my year designing an application that (beautifully) matches small business owners with available government incentives. I am currently the Product Designer at Yo.
