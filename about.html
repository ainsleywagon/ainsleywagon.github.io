Digital Product Designer in San Francisco

I'm a designer / musician / maker / writer interested in creating immersive atmospheres. I was raised by church musicians and schooled by architecture academics.

I got my degree in Architecture where I designed hospitals, built light fixtures, played with Arduinos, and give a lecture about haptic installations. I gravitated to digital products for the potential of rapid iteration and the very intimate way in which we connect to our devices.

Last year I contracted with Pinterest, Yo, and IDEO.org.
I am currently an Experience Designer with Adobe XD.
