Digital Product Designer in San Francisco

Raised by church musicians and schooled by architecture professors. I'm a designer / musician / maker / writer interested in creating immersive atmospheres.

I got my degree in Architecture where I built light fixtures, played with Arduinos, and gave lectures about haptic installations by day while I made music and art with friends by night.
I gravitated to digital products for the potential of rapid iteration and a reverence for the very intimate way we connect to our devices.

Last year I contracted with Pinterest, Yo, and IDEO.org.
I am currently an Experience Designer with Adobe XD.
