OAM

OAM is an experiment in mixing sound, colours, and geometries on the web.

A big theme in my work is exploring the intersection of music and visuals. I was interested to do this in the web browser, and partnered with Varun to make it happen.

OAM is a series of different sonic and visual modes in which you can build shape and sound collages.
