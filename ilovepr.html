ILovePuertoRico.org

Collecting Love Letters to La Isla de Encanta

While in residency for Code for America in San Juan, Puerto Rico, I had the privilege to meet and talk to a lot of Puerto Rican citizens about the challenges facing the island. Outside of our project's focus area, one major concern was the large amounts of Puerto Ricans leaving the island - particularly young people.

With ILovePuertoRico.org, we wanted to build a platform that collected all the little reasons why its citizens loved the island. We wanted a visual wall of love notes detailing why people stayed in Puerto Rico. People loved the premise and the messages we collected were amazing. From family to its natural beauty, to the work ethic of the people - the sentiments we uncovered were a powerful testament to the spirit of the island.

I worked with my developer teammate to spin up this platform, which has since been forked for many other feel-good purposes (birthday greetings are a particularly popular use.) This was my first stab at reading and writing in Jade, and I had a lot of fun building something that was an easy and straightforward way for people to communicate positive feelings about their country.

You can read much more detailed documentation about the design and development of this app here at our Code for America team blog.
