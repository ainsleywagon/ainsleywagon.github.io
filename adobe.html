Adobe

Assets Shared Pages

Designing how people share and showcase their work.

One of my projects at Adobe has been to redesign the share pages for when someone sends an asset (file, folder, or library) to someone else, and wants them to be able to browse and view it publicly.

This meant a deep dive into how best to display assets, in a way that would showcase the content but be browsable for quick access.

I developed a language that kept consistency with existing workflows but allowed more room for the content to shine, while still building in ways to sort, filter, and navigate.
