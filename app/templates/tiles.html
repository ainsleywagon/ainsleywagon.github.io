Tiles

A pretty picture-making game that everyone wins!

This is a project I co-developed with Imanol Aranzadi - just for the fun of it. Inspired by children's wooden blocks that have nice pictures on every side, that no matter how you arrange them make up a nice pattern.

I developed the visual design, drawing the tiles in Illustrator which looked good in any arrangement - random or planned. As I played with it, I realized the visual similarity to quilts and folk art, which reveals the strong Appalachian design influences from my Kentucky roots.

It's interesting to me how simple designs reveal complex influences. Go check it out and edit the title for your creation!
